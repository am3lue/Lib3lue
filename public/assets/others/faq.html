Common Questions

Q: How do I create an account?

A: Click on the 'Login' button and select 'Sign Up' to create your new account.

Q: How can I access the books?

A: Navigate to the 'Books' section and browse our collection. You need to be logged in to download books.

Q: Is the service free?

A: Yes, basic access is completely free. Premium features may require a subscription.

Q: How can I contact support?

A: You can reach us through the 'Contact' page or email us at support@3lue.com

Q: Can I contribute to the library?

A: Yes! We welcome contributions. Please check our guidelines in the 'Docs' section.

Q: What formats are supported?

A: We support PDF, EPUB, and MOBI formats for all our books.
